🎂 About the Age Calculator

This tool calculates your exact age based on your date of birth, giving you the result in years, months, days, total weeks, and total days.

It's useful for official documents, medical check-ins, job applications, or simply satisfying your curiosity!

🧮 How to Use

  1. Enter your date of birth in the first field.
  2. Optionally set a target date in the second field (defaults to today).
  3. Click "Calculate Age" for instant results.

💡 Did You Know?

  • The average human lifespan is roughly 28,000 days.
  • Leap years add an extra day every 4 years (roughly), affecting age calculations.
  • Some cultures count age from conception rather than birth.

❓ FAQ
