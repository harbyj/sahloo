📌 What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. The symbol "%" means "per hundred." Percentages are used in everyday life for discounts, taxes, tips, exam scores, and much more.

Formula: Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

🧮 How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose the calculation type using the tabs above.
  2. Enter the required numbers in the fields.
  3. Click Calculate to get an instant result.

💡 Practical Examples

  • Shopping discount: A $200 item at 15% off → save $30, pay $170
  • Exam score: 45 correct out of 60 → 75%
  • Sales tax: $100 + 15% VAT = $115
  • Salary raise: $5,000 + 10% raise = $5,500

❓ FAQ

20% of 150 = 0.20 × 150 = 30.
Percentage increase = ((New − Old) / Old) × 100. For example, from 100 to 120 is a 20% increase.
Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100. For example, 45 out of 60 = 75%.
If a final price after 20% discount is 80, the original is 80 / 0.80 = 100.
