Discount Calculator

Find final price after discount and your savings

🏷️ Smart Shopping Guide

During sales seasons, stores use stacked discounts to create the illusion of bigger savings. Use this calculator to find the true final price before you buy.

💡 Types of Discounts

  • Flat discount: A percentage deducted directly from the original price
  • Stacked discounts: e.g. "20% off then extra 10%" — this is NOT 30%!
  • Buy one get one half price: Effectively 25% off per item
  • Coupon code: Usually applied after the listed discount
