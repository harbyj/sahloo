🧾 About the Bill Splitter

After a great meal the question always comes: "How much does each person owe?" This tool instantly calculates the fair share per person, with or without a tip.

💡 Tipping Guide

  • 10% — Satisfactory service
  • 15% — Good service
  • 20% — Excellent service
  • Some restaurants add a service charge automatically — check your bill before tipping!

❓ FAQ

Tip = Bill × Tip% / 100, then divided equally per person.
Arab countries: 10–15%. US: 15–20%. Europe: 5–10% or none.
This tool splits equally. Enter each person's sub-total separately for custom splits.
