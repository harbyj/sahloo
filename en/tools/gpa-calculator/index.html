🎓 GPA Grading Systems

  • 4.0 System: Standard in USA and Canada. A=4.0, B=3.0, C=2.0, D=1.0
  • 5.0 System: Common in Saudi Arabia and Gulf universities. A+=5.0, A=4.75
  • Percentage (100): Used in Egypt, Morocco, Jordan and more. 90–100 = Excellent

💡 How to Use

  1. Select your university's grading system
  2. Add each course with its grade and credit hours
  3. Click Calculate to see your cumulative GPA

❓ FAQ

The 4.0 scale is standard in the US. A=4.0, B=3.0, C=2.0, D=1.0, F=0. An A+ is also 4.0.
On a 4.0 scale: 3.5+ is excellent, 3.0–3.5 is good, 2.5–3.0 is average. Requirements vary by institution.
GPA = Sum of (Grade Points × Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours. This calculator uses weighted averaging.
