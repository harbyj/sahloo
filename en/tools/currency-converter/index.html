💱 How Does the Converter Work?

This tool fetches live exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API.com every time the page loads. The rates are mid-market rates updated hourly.

For actual money transfers, your bank or exchange service will apply their own buying/selling spreads on top of the mid-market rate.

🌍 Supported Currencies

  • GCC: Saudi Riyal (SAR), UAE Dirham (AED), Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD), Qatari Riyal (QAR), Bahraini Dinar (BHD), Omani Rial (OMR)
  • Arab World: Egyptian Pound (EGP), Jordanian Dinar (JOD), Moroccan Dirham (MAD), Tunisian Dinar (TND), Algerian Dinar (DZD), Iraqi Dinar (IQD)
  • Global: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CNY, TRY and 130+ more

💡 Tips for Currency Exchange

  • Always compare rates from multiple banks and exchange services
  • Watch out for hidden fees and commissions
  • Exchange rates fluctuate constantly — check before large transactions
  • Banks typically offer better rates than airport exchange booths
