🔒 Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Sahloo is built with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

What We Collect

Nothing. All calculations on Sahloo happen entirely in your browser (client-side JavaScript). No data is ever sent to our servers.

  • We do not require registration or login
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not use advertising networks
  • We do not sell or share any data

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, anonymized analytics (e.g. page view counts only) to understand which tools are most useful. No personally identifiable information is ever collected.

External Services

The Currency Converter tool fetches live exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API.com. When you use this tool, your browser makes a request to their API. Please refer to their privacy policy for details.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Google may log font requests per their privacy policy.

Children

Sahloo does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date of the last update will be noted here.
