Why Support Sahloo?

Sahloo is completely free — no ads, no sign-up, no tracking. Every tool is built for the Arabic-speaking community to make everyday calculations easier.

Running a website costs money (servers, domain, development time). Your support, no matter how small, helps us keep all tools free and build new ones.

What We'll Never Do

  • We will never show intrusive ads
  • We will never sell your data
  • We will never charge for basic tools
